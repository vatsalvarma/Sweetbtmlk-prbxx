What’s Special About This Drink?

Sweet Buttermilk is a softer, comforting take on traditional buttermilk. While classic buttermilk refreshes with a savory touch, this version adds a mild natural sweetness that makes it feel soothing and satisfying.

It’s the perfect balance between nourishment and indulgence — light on the stomach, yet pleasantly energizing. At Shake & Gulp, we keep it simple and honest, just like it’s made at home. Because real ingredients naturally settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.

Health Benefits

  • Helps cool the body naturally
  • Provides light energy without heaviness
  • Easy to digest and refreshing
  • Supports hydration during warm days
  • Gentle on the stomach

Who Should Drink This?

Sweet Buttermilk is enjoyable for everyone as a refreshing daily drink.

It’s especially helpful for people who experience:

  • Body heat or summer fatigue
  • Mild tiredness or low energy
  • Sensitivity to spicy or heavy foods
  • A desire for something soothing yet refreshing
  • A need for light hydration

A sweet, cooling drink your body will welcome.

Emotional Connect

Sweet Buttermilk feels like a pause in a busy day — a moment of calm, a touch of comfort. It brings back memories of simple homemade treats, shared in the quiet afternoons of summer.

Shake & Gulp captures that gentle joy and puts it in your hands — familiar, comforting, and made with care.
